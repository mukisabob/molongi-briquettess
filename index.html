Our Commitment to Sustainability

Environmental Impact

Molongi Briquettess reduces deforestation by replacing wood and charcoal with eco-friendly briquettess made from agricultural waste.

Clean Energy for Communities

We offer affordable, smokeless fuel alternatives that improve air quality and health in local households.

Waste to Wealth

Our briquettess are made from banana peelings, sawdust, and other agricultural waste—turning trash into treasure.

Empowering Rural Youth & Women

We work with local communities, especially women and youth, to provide jobs and training in briquettess production.
